THE BACKWATERS OF ALLEPPEY

Known as the 'Venice of the East', Alleppey in Kerala offers serene backwater experiences. The intricate network of canals, lagoons, and lakes invites visitors to relax and explore on houseboats. The backwaters provide a unique way to witness rural life and the rich biodiversity of the region.

Priya Raj
12 - 4 - 2023

JAIPUR: THE PINK CITY

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its rich history and vibrant culture. The city, adorned with pink-colored buildings, houses majestic palaces like the Hawa Mahal and the City Palace. Jaipur's bustling bazaars, historic forts, and warm hospitality make it a must-visit destination.

Arjun Mehta
5 - 5 - 2023

THE TAJ MAHAL, AGRA

One of the most iconic monuments in the world, the Taj Mahal in Agra is a symbol of love and architectural marvel. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this white marble mausoleum stands as a testament to timeless beauty and craftsmanship. The surrounding gardens and reflecting pool add to its charm.

Sneha Kapoor
